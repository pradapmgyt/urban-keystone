About

Welcome to Urban Keystone Architectural Studio — a creative and client-focused architectural practice with 5 years of experience in delivering thoughtful, functional, and inspiring spaces. Over the years, we have successfully handled a wide range of residential, commercial, and institutional projects, creating designs that balance aesthetics, practicality, and sustainability. At Urban Keystone Architectural Studio, we believe every project should reflect the vision and lifestyle of our clients. Our services include architectural planning, interior design, space optimization, 3D visualization and walkthroughs, and detailed material selection to ensure every element is carefully curated. From concept development to execution, we provide end-to-end design solutions tailored to each project’s unique requirements.
We also prioritize quality and transparency through regular site visits and factory visits, ensuring proper coordination, workmanship, and material standards throughout the project journey. Our hands-on approach allows us to maintain design accuracy while delivering projects efficiently and professionally. Driven by creativity, innovation, and attention to detail, Urban Keystone Architectural Studio continues to create meaningful environments that inspire people and enhance everyday living. We are committed to transforming ideas into timeless architectural experiences with precision, passion, and excellence

Ar. Santhosh

Co-Founder & Architect


Santhosh believes every project should respond contextually to its environment, creating spaces that are functional, meaningful, and experienced in unique ways. His design approach carefully considers climate, sustainability, and modern living to deliver cohesive architectural solutions. Santhosh graduated from School of Architecture CIET in 2021 and gained professional experience working with Noor Dasmesh Singh in Chandigarh during his practical training. He has also worked across Chennai and Bangalore and recently served as a Project Architect at Casagrand Builders, bringing valuable industry expertise and practical knowledge to every project he undertakes.


Ar. Lavanya

Co-Founder & Architect


Lavanya believes in creating spaces that balance functionality, aesthetics, and thoughtful planning to enhance everyday living. With strong knowledge in colour theory, communication, and design efficiency, she brings a detail-oriented and creative approach to every project. She graduated from MNM Jain School of Architecture in 2020 and gained experience with Kulkarni Associates in Bangalore. She also worked with Casagrand Builders in Chennai as an overall project in-house architect, contributing to planning, coordination, and execution. Her approach focuses on creating holistic designs that reflect both the firm’s aesthetic vision and the client’s personality and lifestyle.
